# Working with widgets

Widgets in BCMS are reusable building blocks that are used inside of [entry's content area](/inside-bcms/entries).

---

## Creating and editing a widget

To create a widget, click on **Create new widget**. In the next step, fill in the label, and optional description.

To edit a widget, click on the widget you want to edit and update [properties](/inside-bcms/properties), or click on the "Edit widget" to edit its label or description.

---

## Adding properties to a widget

To add properties to your newly created widget, drag and configure properties to the widget area.

---

## Deleting a widget

Deleting a widget is as easy as clicking on the **Delete widget**, inside the edit widget modal.

<Note>
  This will remove the widget from all Entries where it is used, so delete widgets with caution.
</Note>
