# Configuring BCMS

At BCMS, our top priority is providing excellent experience for both developers and website editors.
That includes the configuration process too. Instead of learning a BCMS-specific syntax, with BCMS, it's possible to configure your content management system in-browser.

From setting up the templates and content modeling, over fine-grained user permissions - everything is intuitive, and available in browser.
